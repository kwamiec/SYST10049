Although I was born in Canada I recognize Ghana to be my home country. My Mom and Dad immigrated to Canada in 1986 and 1976 respectively. Ghana is located along the Gulf of Guinea in West Africa, sharing a border with Code d'Ivoire, Burkina Faso and Togo. It is one of the few countries within the West African subregion that do not speak French; as English is one of the official languages of the country. Ghana, also known as the Gold Coast, is one of Africa's leading suppliers of gold. The country has an abundance of resources but is mainly known for its gold, cocoa and oil.
